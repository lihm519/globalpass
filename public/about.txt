# GlobalPass - eSIM Price Comparison Platform

## What We Do
GlobalPass is a real-time price comparison platform for international eSIM data plans. We help travelers find the cheapest eSIM packages by comparing prices from multiple providers including Airalo, Nomad, and others.

## Key Features
- Real-time price comparison across 425+ eSIM packages
- Coverage for 20+ countries worldwide
- Phone compatibility checker
- AI-powered travel assistant
- Updated daily with latest pricing

## How It Works
1. Enter your destination country
2. Browse available eSIM packages
3. Compare prices, data amounts, and validity periods
4. Click to purchase from the provider's website
